About Us

We are a team of five students working together on a project where our mission is to create a website aimed at solving the accommodation challenges faced by students coming to study at Hochschule Fulda in Germany. Recognizing the struggles that many incoming students experience in finding reliable, affordable, and accessible housing, we are developing a platform to streamline the accommodation search process and help them transition smoothly into their new academic life in Fulda.
